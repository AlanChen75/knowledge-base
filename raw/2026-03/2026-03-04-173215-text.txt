紀實攝影感（documentary realism）＋微瑕疵細節＋自然光源。
下面整理一套可直接使用的「真實照片風格提示詞結構」。

---

一、核心結構公式

主體 + 鏡頭描述 + 光線條件 + 細節紋理 + 色彩設定 + 拍攝器材感 + 排除修圖

---

二、萬用真實感 Prompt 模板（人像）

Extreme close-up portrait of [subject], three-quarter view, natural expression.

Hyper-detailed skin texture with visible pores, subtle imperfections, light freckles, faint wrinkles, natural facial hair stubble.

Soft natural window light grazing across the face, realistic shadows, no studio lighting.

Neutral color science, no crushed blacks, natural skin tones.

Shot on 85mm portrait lens, shallow depth of field, organic sharpness, slight digital grain.

Documentary photography realism.

No heavy retouching, no beauty filters, no artificial smoothing.

The image should feel effortless, unposed, authentic, like a casual everyday photo captured in available light.

---

三、強化「真實感」關鍵詞

加入以下元素會大幅提升真實度：

1️⃣ 細節紋理
visible pores
subtle skin texture
tiny scars
uneven skin tone
light sun damage
fine baby hairs
slight sweat

2️⃣ 光線設定
soft window light
harsh side lighting
natural ambient light
overcast daylight
realistic shadow falloff

3️⃣ 鏡頭語言
shot on 50mm / 85mm / 100mm macro
shallow depth of field
eye-level perspective
handheld feel

4️⃣ 反修圖關鍵
no beauty filters
no artificial smoothing
no HDR look
no oversharpening
natural color rendering

---

四、物品真實感版本（例如球、杯子、耳朵）

Extreme macro close-up of a worn object.

Hyper-detailed surface texture with visible dust, scratches, imperfections, and natural wear.

Harsh outdoor side lighting revealing surface depth.

Neutral color science, realistic contrast.

Shot on 100mm macro lens, ultra shallow depth of field.

Documentary realism.

No artificial enhancement, no smoothing, no stylization.

---

五、中文思維邏輯（給 Gemini 或其他模型用）

如果用中文提示，可這樣寫：

超近距離微距特寫
可見毛孔與細微瑕疵
自然窗邊光
無濾鏡
紀實攝影風格
手機隨手拍感
自然色彩科學
輕微數位顆粒
淺景深
不做修圖

---

六、讓 AI 更像「手機拍的」

加入：
realistic smartphone photography style
slight motion imperfection
imperfect framing
casual composition

---

七、關鍵觀念

真正讓照片「像真的」不是：
❌ 超清晰
❌ 超乾淨
❌ 完美皮膚

而是：
✅ 不完美
✅ 光線真實
✅ 紋理存在
✅ 顏色自然
✅ 無人工修飾痕跡
